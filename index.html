Core workflow

Adding a new patient

Follow these steps every time a new patient is referred or schedules their first appointment.

  1. 1
    Navigate to Clients in the left sidebar and select "Add new client"
  2. 2
    Complete the intake form — legal name, date of birth, insurance ID, and emergency contact are required fields
  3. 3
    Send the client portal invite so the patient can complete consent forms digitally before their first visit
  4. 4
    Confirm insurance eligibility using the built-in verification tool — this takes under 60 seconds
